Remarks
A method for driving Mecanum wheeled robots in the polar plane. There are 4 wheels on the robot, arranged so that the front and back wheels are toed in 45 degrees. When looking at the wheels from the top, the roller axles should form an X across the robot.

This is designed to be directly driven by raw vectors.
